Oatmeal Chocolate No-Bake Cookies

Oatmeal chocolate no-bake cookies are a quick and delicious treat that requires no oven time. They combine rich chocolate, creamy peanut butter, and hearty oats for an easy dessert that sets in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • 3 cups quick oats
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine sugar, milk, butter, and cocoa powder.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat.
  3. Let it boil for 1 minute, then remove from heat.
  4. Stir in peanut butter and vanilla until smooth.
  5. Add oats and mix until fully combined.
  6. Drop spoonfuls onto wax paper.
  7. Allow cookies to cool and set before serving.
